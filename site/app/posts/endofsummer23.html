End Of Summer Recap

I feel weird about this summer. As summer is nearing it's end, I am looking back at what I have done. On one hand I feel like I did a lot of stuff, on the other I feel as if I did nothing. Something about myself; I don't like doing nothing, or feeling unproductive. I did A LOT of things, I know it. But I stil don't feel productive. This could be going back to a diagnosis of depression, but in an attempt to not feel depressed, I am going to highlight all that I have dont this summer.

The summer began and I felt relived I was out of school. I though I would be able to golf a lot. I was not able to golf a lot, and have been more occupied with other things. One signifigant thing I did was fix my sprinkler system in my backyard. One of the head was broke when it was ran over by a lawn mower. It broke the head off the T fitting, so that had to be replaced too. This was a 2 day project, as I had the wrong fitting for the tube, and had to go back to the hardware store. Eventually it was fixed, but there is still an issue with the sprinkler system's plumbing. The water faucet is not connected to the wall very well, and may need replaced. This is something bigger and will need more time to do, as I will need to get some help doing this.

Also this summer I took a college class. The class I took was PSYCH 101, Introduction to Psychology. I enjoyed my time taking it and learned a lot. Not only did I learn about psychology, but I also learned about the whole college academic enviorment (which I will later elaborate on). Personally, I did not find it much different than high school. However it was during a summer term and a hybrid learning class, so that means there was less people in the class. I think I did good for my first college class, as I finished with an A. I also learned about APA format, as one assignment doing a report in APA format. I prefer MLA still, as it is more paper efficent, thus ecologically friendly. The professor was really nice, and when I went to Rose Hulman, she was understanding of my situation and gave me an extention. Overall it was a pretty good class and experince.

I have mentioned in a previous post how I am went to Rose Hulman Project Select. Firstly, I will say that my counselor Henry, was very nice, and seem to be the nicer of the 6. There were 5 academic days there and each day we did something different. On top of that there were fun activities that were hosted each day. If you want to find out more about my experince there, you can read my post here. One thing I will note however is that this also had an emphasis on social things, as there were mandatory social things. Some were fun (jack box games). Others were not (soccer on turf in 90 degree heat). But I was able to talk to other people interested in similar things, so that was nice.

I also have been making some really good progress on my personal robot. I have most of the chasis built and all of the electronic circuts built. I need to work on the code some.
